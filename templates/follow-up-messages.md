# Follow-Up Message Templates

Three templated messages for post-event founder follow-up. Each message uses personalization tokens in [brackets]. Replace all tokens with specific details from the startup's validation planner and 30-day plan before sending.

---

## Message 1: 2-Day Check-In

**Subject:** Quick follow-up from Founder Jam

---

Hey [Founder Name],

Great working with you at Founder Jam on [Day]. I wanted to check in while everything is fresh.

You committed to [specific commitment from validation planner] in the first 2 days. How's that going?

Quick reminder of your immediate priorities:
- [Week 1 action 1]
- [Week 1 action 2]
- [Week 1 action 3]

If you hit a wall on any of these, reply and I'll point you in the right direction.

[Facilitator Name]

---

## Message 2: 2-Week Milestone

**Subject:** 2-week check -- how's [specific metric] looking?

---

Hey [Founder Name],

Two weeks since Founder Jam. By now you should have:
- [Week 2 milestone 1]
- [Week 2 milestone 2]

How are the numbers looking? Your target was [specific number from 30-day plan].

If you're ahead of pace -- great, keep pushing.
If you're behind -- that's normal. The question is why. Reply with a quick update and I'll share what I've seen work for other founders in your situation.

[Facilitator Name]

---

## Message 3: 2-Month Validation Gate

**Subject:** Decision time -- Green, Yellow, or Red?

---

Hey [Founder Name],

It's been 2 months since Founder Jam. Time for the honest check-in.

Your North Star target was [specific metric]: [specific number].

Where did you land?

- Green (70%+): You've found signal. Time to double down and build systems around what's working.
- Yellow (40-70%): There's something here but it needs tuning. What's the one metric furthest from target?
- Red (<40%): Time to revisit the fundamentals. Was the ICP right? Was the channel right? Was the offer right?

No judgment either way -- the framework exists so you can make the call without emotion.

Reply with your color and I'll send you specific next steps for that path.

[Facilitator Name]

---

## Personalization Tokens Reference

| Token | Source |
|-------|--------|
| `[Founder Name]` | First name only |
| `[Day]` | Day of week (e.g., Saturday) |
| `[specific commitment from validation planner]` | The 2-day commitment they read aloud |
| `[Week 1 action 1-3]` | From their 30-day plan, Week 1 rows |
| `[Week 2 milestone 1-2]` | From their 30-day plan, Week 2 success criteria |
| `[specific number from 30-day plan]` | The quantified target they set (e.g., 50 landing page visitors) |
| `[specific metric]` | Their North Star metric (Growth Track) or hypothesis metric (Product Track) |
| `[Facilitator Name]` | Your first name |

## Notes

- Send Message 1 within 48 hours of the event, ideally the same evening or next morning.
- Send Message 2 exactly 14 days post-event. Set a calendar reminder before you leave.
- Send Message 3 approximately 60 days post-event.
- Keep all three messages in plain text. No formatting, no bullet-heavy walls of text.
- If a founder replies before a scheduled message, skip that message or acknowledge their update in the next one.
